Salmons

This a simple recipe to achieve a crispy freshly baked salmon.

Ingredients

  • Preferred seasonings, Butter, Lemons, Airfryer, and great attitude

Steps

  1. Season the salmon with spices
  2. preheat airfryer to 400 degrees for 8 min
  3. Slice lemons vertically aprox 1/8 thick
  4. line airfryer tray with aluminium foil
  5. lay 3-4 lemons on the tray, then place the salmon on top of them
  6. cut the the butter intoi small tablespoon sizes
  7. place serveral pieces of butter on top
  8. insert untill the timer has gone off
  9. reset the timer for 4-5 min at 375 to finish cooking
  10. when the salmon is finished, snap a pic bc the camera eats first!
