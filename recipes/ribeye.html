Ribeye

This a simple recipe to achieve a juicy medium grassfed ribeye.

Ingredients

  • grassfed ribeye, butter, salt and pepper, cast iron or ss pan, and great attitude

Steps

  1. have steak salted and in the fridge for 12 hours
  2. heat up pan to 400 degrees and use the butter for high smoke point
  3. steak from fridge and sear for 90 sec
  4. 90 sec flip once to the other side
  5. that has finished if the fat hasnt rendererd on the sides flip steak to desired side for 60 sec
  6. the steak is seared on all 4 sides, lower heat to med and let sit for 3-4 min
  7. the time has finished remove steak and let rest for 8 min
  8. steak is done resting slice with the grain and enjoy
