Vietnamese Coffee

This a simple recipe to achieve a a smooth, strong, and delicious coffe to start the day.

Ingredients

  • instant coffe mix, a way to heat up water, coffe strainer, condensed milk, and patience

Steps

  1. Start by boiling water
  2. Preheat the filter and cup by running water through them both
  3. Remove the metal filter and pour 1 heaping tablespoon
  4. Gently screw the filter clockwise unti its snug, and then twist the filter about 1/8 a turn
  5. Pour water throughh filter to make it wet so the coffe ground can expand a bit
  6. fill the filter all the way and let it drip, too loose and you have runny brown water, too tight and nothing will drip
  7. For condensed milk add your preferred amount
  8. when it has finihsed dripping you are free to enjoy, if you prefer it iced, simply add ice!
